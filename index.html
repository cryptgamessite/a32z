Blockchain is eating the world

Blockchain is eating the world. We believe that blockchain-based crypto assets will show explosive growth. Crypto assets will become part of financial assets, same as stocks, funds and bonds. At the same time, non-standard assets, also known as Non-Fungible Tokens (NFT), will continue to emerge and will profoundly change our economic life. A32Z believes that discovering, investing in and operating NFT is quite valuable and exciting. We will focus on this goal and look forward to working with people who share the same beliefs to witness the next future.
